About Medease Travels

Medease Travels is a trusted partner for Kenyans seeking world-class medical care in India. We specialize in connecting patients to the finest healthcare facilities across India, ensuring access to affordable, high-quality medical services tailored to diverse needs. Our dedicated team provides comprehensive support throughout the medical journey, from selecting the right hospital to post-treatment care, making the entire process seamless and stress-free.

Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred medical tourism company for Kenyan patients, providing unparalleled access to world-class healthcare facilities and transforming lives through quality and affordability.

Mission

To simplify medical travel for Kenyan patients by offering comprehensive services, fostering partnerships with leading healthcare providers in India, and ensuring a seamless, affordable, and compassionate journey to better health.

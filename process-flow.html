Patient - Process Flow

Step 1

📞 Reach out to the Medease Hospital Travel Agency. Our case management team will respond within 24 hours.

⬇️

Step 2

📄 The designated case manager will handle your queries and remain accessible 24/7 via phone, WhatsApp. They will work to understand your needs, gather all necessary medical documents electronically, and share them with Medease consultants for further review.

⬇️

Step 3

💸 Medease doctors will offer treatment options tailored to your medical reports, including estimated costs and the duration of your stay at Medease facilities. Additionally, we arrange conference calls between you and a Medease consultant to ensure you fully understand the proposed treatment plan.

⬇️

Step 4

✈️ If you choose to travel, you will need to provide copies of the passports for both the patient and the attendant. We will then issue an invitation letter and assist you in obtaining a medical visa.

⬇️

Step 5

🎫 The patient should share their air tickets with us, and we will arrange complimentary airport transfers from the airport to the hospital. Following this, we will schedule appointments with Medease doctors.

⬇️

Step 6

🏥 Proceed with the procedure or treatment and recover at the hospital.

⬇️

Step 7

🧳 Tourism option: You can either stay for sightseeing in the surrounding area or depart for home.

⬇️

Step 8

👨‍⚕️ Stay in touch with your local physician and keep us updated on your medical condition and records to ensure continuous care.

☑️
